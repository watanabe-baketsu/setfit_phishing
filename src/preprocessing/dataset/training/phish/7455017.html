The benefits of Global Entry include expedited border crossings, reduced wait times at airports, no processing lines, and no need to fill out customs paperwork. You will also get TSA Pre✓® Eligibility, which allows you to skip the majority of the airport line, allows you to keep your shoes and belt on while going through airport security, keep your liquids and laptop in your bag, saving you time before a flight. Upon landing, you will no longer need to fill out any customs paperwork at all. The automated Global Entry kiosks will already have all of your flight, passport, and personal details saved. You will also skip the airport line upon landing, so you can go straight from the plane, to the automated kiosk, to the airport exit. At the airport exit, you will also have an expedited line, and not need to go through the additional customs check. Global Entry is available in the US, UK, Germany, Netherlands, South Korea, Taiwan, Panama, Mexico, and members can enjoy expedited processing in these countries also.
