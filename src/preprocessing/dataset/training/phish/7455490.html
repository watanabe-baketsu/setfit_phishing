Note: E-mail and telephone fraud

Cybercriminals are currently exploiting the uncertainties in the population regarding the coronavirus emergency.
They send false e-mails by impersonating as official authorities such as the Federal Office of Public Health (FOPH) with the aim of spreading malware or obtaining personal information.
Similar cases have been reported by telephone.
We ask you to delete such e-mail messages or to ignore telephone calls.
