Explore our research

A member of the Russell Group, we are committed to strengthening our position as one of the world’s premier institutions for inspirational and life-changing research.

Our research is focused around seven themes which align our academic strengths to best meet the grand scientific, social and environmental challenges of our time.

Good on paper

Grammar lessons taught in primary schools in England seemingly don’t help children to learn to write.

Hardest hit

In its first year, the impact of the pandemic on England and Wales was far worse for some than others.

Negative energy

Research on children's consumption of energy drinks suggests a link to health and behavioural issues.

Oil and water

Re-wetting peatlands supports biodiversity without affecting oil palm farmers' livelihoods.

Connect to projects that enable brilliant minds to break barriers, overcome limitations and think differently to solve the challenges of the world today.

Supporting those in crisis

Our Equal Access Fund supports asylum-seeking students, including those affected by the crisis in Ukraine or who have recently fled Afghanisan.
