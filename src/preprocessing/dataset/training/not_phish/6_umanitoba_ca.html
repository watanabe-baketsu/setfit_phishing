Continued health and safety protocols

UM's current health and safety protocols continue to apply until the end of the Winter Term (May 3):

  • KN95 masks must continue to be worn in all indoor spaces on UM campuses. Please refer to the masking section on the Health and Safety Protocols page for a list of mask distribution locations.
  • UM will continue to require full (double) vaccination for all staff, faculty, students and visitors to UM campuses.
  • We strongly advise you to follow UM’s existing self-isolation protocols. 
  • Please continue to report positive COVID-19 cases on our campuses to help track COVID-19 at UM.
  • Stay home when you are sick. Employees should connect with their supervisor to make required arrangements. Students should work directly with instructors to seek reasonable accommodation. 

  • For more information visit the COVID-19 website
