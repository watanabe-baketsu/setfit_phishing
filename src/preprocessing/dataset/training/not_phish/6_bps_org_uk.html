Changes to our Privacy Policy

We’re making some changes to our Privacy Policy, effective immediately.

What’s changing :

  • We are changing the way we can use our members data to inform you about any important and exciting fundraising activities.
  • Distribution groups provided by the BPS are being used more often by non-members, we have changed our privacy policy to let our users know what data is collected and processed when joining one of these groups.
  • We are updating the way we make changes to this policy.

If you have any queries about the changes or how the BPS process your data, please contact dataprotection@bps.org.uk.

By continuing to use our website you are agreeing to the terms of our Privacy Policy.
