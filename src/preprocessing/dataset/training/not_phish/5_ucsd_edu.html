UC San Diego Celebrates
Women’s “Herstory” Month

This March, we commemorate Women’s “Herstory” Month as well as the 25th anniversary of UC San Diego’s Women’s Center. Campus community members are invited to celebrate the month with a series of events, ranging from a conversation with author and cultural critic Roxane Gay to a feminist bingo night and a women in law panel.
