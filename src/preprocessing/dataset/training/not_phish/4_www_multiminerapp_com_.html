What is Multiminer?

MultiMiner is a cryptocurrency mining application available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Its simple interface makes it easy to choose which cryptocurrency to mine by detecting the hardware you have available on your device.

MultiMiner supports mining with graphical processing units (GPUs), application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs), and field-programmable gate array (FPGA) circuits.

MultiMiner supports a range of mining algorithms, including but not limited to SHA256, Scrypt, CryptoNight, Ethash, Equihash, Pascal, Ethash, Keccak, Quark, Scrypt-Jane, and X11-15. This makes it possible to mine Bitcoin, Ethereum, Monero, Litecoin, Zcash, and many more.

You can use MultiMiner's configurable strategies to automatically switch to mining the most profitable cryptocurrency.

Alternatively, if you want to make the decision manually, you can have MultiMiner alert you when there is a more profitable coin than the one you're currently mining.

It's also possible to connect MultiMiner with smartphone monitoring software so you can check up on and control your mining rigs no matter where you are.

Advanced users will appreciate the ability to use a stratum proxy to point other miners to their network, effectively creating their own mining pool.
