What is Situational Crime Prevention?

Situational crime prevention is a crime reduction strategy used by law enforcement that posits that criminal acts may be deterred by environmental changes.  It focuses on the setting for crime, rather than those committing criminal acts.  The main goal is to make criminal acts less attractive, and requires assistance from public and private entities.
