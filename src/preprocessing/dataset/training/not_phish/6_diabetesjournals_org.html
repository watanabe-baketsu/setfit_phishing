    American Diabetes Association: Professional Publications

    The American Diabetes Association® (ADA) is committed to publishing the most timely, innovative research and information for professionals who specialize in diabetes research, treatment, education, and care. Search a continually updated database of 70,000+ articles from the ADA’s high-impact journals, books, monographs, meeting abstracts, clinical guidelines, and multimedia.

    Diabetes Core Update Podcast

    Diabetes Core Update is a monthly audio podcast devoted to presenting the latest clinically relevant articles from ADA’s four scholarly journals: Diabetes, Diabetes Care, Clinical Diabetes and Diabetes Spectrum. Listen now.
