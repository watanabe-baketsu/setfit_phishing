Whether you’re producing your passion project or growing a podcast business it has never been easier. Spreaker is the one-stop shop for podcast hosting, creation, distribution and monetization.

Ready to record?

Start your show today. Like, right now. The Spreaker Studio app gets your podcast up and running quickly – it has everything you need to record and publish your podcast from pretty much everywhere.

UPLOAD AND DISTRIBUTE

Upload and distribute in one click.

Post your podcast far and wide. Spreaker’s streamlined distribution system sends your episodes to the top podcast platforms in just one click, including Apple Podcasts, Spotify, iHeart Podcast Network, Google Podcasts, and more.
