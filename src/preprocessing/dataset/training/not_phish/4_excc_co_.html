Anonymous

These days privacy is one of the most precious values, and it’s in a constant state of danger. When you’re online, everyone tries to gain as much info about you as possible.

Reasons for this are numerous, but generally speaking you should never trust anyone as nosy as phishers and banking services. ExchangeCoin makes a stand and aims to provide high-quality anonymous services, so you wouldn’t have to fill out any unnecessary personal info forms ever again. EXCC aims to support fair exchange of goods and services, no matter who and where you are.

Decentralised

ExchangeCoin is a decentralised cryptocurrency in full meaning of the definition. As an asset, it’s spread around the world in various, fluctuating quantities. No person or organisation holds the majority of it.

The team behind ExchangeCoin consists of true decentralisation believers, and that shows in our projects. After an extended period of time spent gaining experience, we’re strong on our way to create and launch a true decentralised exchange. Expect more info in the coming months!

Security

Our strong beliefs set our course, but it’s the technical know-how that paves our way. Without strong security systems, none of the things we want to accomplish would be possible. That’s why we’ve decided on cooperating with a truly experienced team of coders, who also share our ideals and want to create the safest cryptocurrency services to be found on the web.

Mining & Staking

We stay uncompromising in our actions. That’s why in the process of ExchangeCoin’s conception we decided to combine Proof of Work and Proof of Stake systems. We’ve done that as one of the first cryptocurrency projects to guarantee that the EXCC stays practically impossible to hack.

Mining and Staking EXCC

EXCC is one of the first cryptocurrencies which combines Proof Of Work with Proof Of Stake. This makes ExchangeCoin almost impossible to hack.

70% Proof of Work
30% Proof of Stake

Proof Of Work
To mine EXCC all you need is a modern GFX card. You can mine EXCC using at least 4GB GFX card (AMD, GeForce, etc.).

Our coin is ASIC resistant so we’ve decided to give full control of the network to the people instead of big players on the market.

Mining EXCC is easy, just visit http://pool.excc.co, download your miner and you are good to go.

Proof Of Stake
This is an extra security layer which makes 51% attacks almost impossible to perform.

To stake EXCC you are required to use Exilibrium wallet or have knowledge about using cryptocurrencies via Terminal.

Staking makes EXCC network stronger so we all benefit from it! Join us at https://stakepool.excc.co
