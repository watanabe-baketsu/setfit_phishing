Binance Jersey has ceased operations.
Dear Visitor,
The Binance Jersey crypto currency exchange is now closed.
If you believe that you may have had crypto assets and / or a GBP or EUR balance(s) with the Jersey exchange at the date of its closure (31st December, 2020 at 23:59 UTC), please contact Customer Service Team with details of your holding and the Binance Customer Service Team will be pleased to assist you.
Subject as provided below, all such assets remaining on the Jersey exchange at the date of its closure are safely held by another member of the Binance Group. The procedure to be followed (including the requirements to be satisfied) by former clients of Binance Jersey in connection with seeking the retrieval of fiat funds and / or amounts of crypto assets shall be as reasonably determined by the Binance Group from time to time. Confirmation of the prevailing procedure and requirements(including the applicable transaction fees) will be provided upon contacting Customer Service Team.
Please kindly note that any crypto-balances which were insufficient to cover the relevant blockchain transaction fee which would have been incurred to transfer such balances at the closure of Binance Jersey (‘dust’), are regrettably lost to the clients concerned. The aggregate of all such balances (or a GBP sum equivalent thereto) will be donated by Binance Jersey shortly after its closure to the Blockchain Charity Foundation (www.binance.charity/about), a non-profit organisation dedicated to achieving global sustainable development by unlocking the power of blockchain.*
The Binance Group extends its gratitude to all clients of Binance Jersey for their loyal support.
Best wishes,
The Binance Jersey Team
If you experience any issues or have any questions regarding withdrawing your coin/ funds from the https://binance.je, please do not hesitate to reach out to support@binance.je
