Put the power of speedy giving in the hands of your donors!

Donorbox Text to Give is super easy to set up and lets your donors give by sending a simple text message from their smartphones! Ideal for on-the-spot donation drives, church services, and conferences.

Our Repeat Donation feature makes it hassle-free to give again. Just one text message - no need to refill the donation form!

Here’s how it works:

  • Donors send a text with your campaign ID
  • Donors receive a text message with the link to your campaign page
  • Donors fill the donation form to complete the donation
  • To repeat a donation, donors simply send another text message
