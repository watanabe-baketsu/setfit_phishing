OU Norman Weather Alert: Online Classes and Remote Work March 11

Due to inclement weather, the OU Norman campus will transition to online classes and remote work Friday, March 11. Weather is not expected to impact internet connectivity and power. Employees able to work remotely should, and essential employees should report to campus and follow their current schedules.
