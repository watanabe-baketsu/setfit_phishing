Meet Our Faculty

Our faculty are world-renowned experts, and trusted advisers to our students, public health leaders, and the public.

Maria-Elena Figueroa

Associate Scientist
Health, Behavior and Society

Maria-Elena Figueroa, PhD '96, researches health behavior and applied communication worldwide, and directs CCP’s Global Program on Water and Hygiene.

Sukon Kanchanaraksa

Senior Scientist
Epidemiology

Sukon Kanchanaraksa, PhD ’86, MHS ’79, leads a team of instructional designers in the Center for Teaching and Learning to support academic and continuing educational activities.

Kayla Tormohlen

Assistant Scientist
Health Policy and Management

Johannes Thrul
