SFU ArtsLIVE

SFU ArtsLIVE is on every week through March 2022 with free, mid-day performances and activations across Burnaby and Surrey campuses. Join us in celebrating a diversity of artistic voices through music, dance, and film this Spring term. Check out the full schedule online and make plans for next week!
