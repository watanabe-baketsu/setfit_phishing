Reminder

 Omniwallet and Watch Only Addresses are Never Involved with Escrow/Recovery services. If you are being asked to open/use Omniwallet as part of an Escrow or Recovery Process you are being SCAMMED. Stop all Communication and contact your local law enforecment. If you have any questions you can contact Omniwallet support below.

If you did not create the wallet you are logging into, you do not own it or any funds within. Do not accept a Wallet/Wallet ID from anyone else as payment for anything. Always create your own wallet and have funds sent to an address you created and control.

Never Share Your Password with anyone. Once someone has access to your wallet they can download a backup/export of any private keys associated with your wallet addresses. Once a private key is compromised any funds on those addresses can never be secured again regardless of password changes or 2fa setup.

RE: Unconfirmed/Pending transactions Aug 10 - Aug 16
Unfortunately we have discovered a bug which was causing the fee estimation to report/use fees lower than expected over the past few days for new transactions. We have already pushed a fix to correct the functionality going forward. While transactions that went out in the last few days should go through eventually it could take a few days for them to get confirmed on the blockchain.
We sincerely apologize to our users and are going to try pushing the transactions manually to one of the mining groups to try and get them confirmed a bit quicker.
A quick note on Bitcoin Fork / Bitcoin Cash.
At present, Omniwallet will not be offering native support for Bitcoin Cash. After the fork, we will be following the Bitcoin-Core network/chain.
Users will always be able to export their address private keys at anytime (before or after the fork).
Exported Address private keys can then be used with a Bitcoin Cash compatible wallet/service.
