Preeminence

Florida State University has been officially designated as a preeminent research university in the state by the Florida Legislature as a result of having met a set of rigorous benchmarks.

Our four-year graduation rate is 74 percent, placing FSU first in the State University System of Florida and in the top 10 nationally among public universities.

Student Stars

Florida State University recognizes its most accomplished students as “Student Stars.” Students are selected for this designation after achieving distinction in their academic area or following significant service to the university.
