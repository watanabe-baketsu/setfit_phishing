Virtual IBAN

We are proud to offer a service of Virtual IBAN (vIBAN) to our users. A virtual IBAN is an IBAN (International Bank Account Number) reference issued by a bank which will enable users to deposit euro on their vIBAN and receive a tokenized representation of the deposited amount. Also, users will be able to deposit fiat and use it to buy crypto. Exciting, isn't it?

Loopring integration

Ethereum recently experienced uncharacteristically high transaction fees due to network congestion. The faster you want your transaction processed, the higher the fee you must pay. This makes it hard to justify the smaller, more frequent kinds of payments, like those you can make with your Eidoo Card. To ensure the Eidoo Card experience is smooth and enjoyable we integrated with Loopring's zkRollup, which processes approx. 2,025 trades per second off-chain. It not only reduces gas consumption and brings overall settlement costs to only fractions of a cent, but also the performance of underlying blockchains no longer affects the speed of trades.
