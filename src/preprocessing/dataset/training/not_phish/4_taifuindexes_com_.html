Description:
The TaiFu™ 30 Index is a market capitalization weighted index of the largest 30 cryptocurrencies for that day. Each day, a new top 30 is selected to calculate the index. It represents the largest 30 cryptocurrencies on a given day.

 

NOTE: We have a lot of folks asking us for API data, historical data, charting capabilities, etc for the TaiFu™ Indexes. Our goal is to release a newer version of the TaiFu™ Indexes in early 2019 with many of these requested features.  Thank you for your patience.
