Where opportunity is center court

As Indiana’s premier urban research institution, where you can choose an IU or Purdue degree, we’re asking questions and making discoveries. In 2020 alone, our researchers were awarded $633 million in external research funds to move Indy and the world forward.

Our values

Diversity, equity & inclusion

We are all-in to promote an inclusive and accessible campus that is community oriented, internationally conscious, and an advocate for the rights of our students. We do this by asking tough, student-centered questions and then consistently working to improve, through anti-racism initiatives, honoring the heritage of Native peoples, taking action locally and globally, and so much more.

Student success

We promote student learning and success in everything we do. Our students are our partners as we focus on their personal and professional development. Our location in Indianapolis provides valuable opportunities our students can only get here.

Educational excellence

We set high standards for our faculty and staff, so we invest in them. They return advancements in health and life sciences and accelerate innovation and discovery through research and creative activity. All the while, they provide excellence in teaching, scholarship, and service to our students.

Community engagement

We’re committed to strengthening the bond we have forged with our community. As a leader in fostering collaborative relationships, we strive to make consistent intellectual, economic, and cultural contributions through community partnerships.

Safety

We care for our community’s health and safety. As home to the largest school of medicine in the nation and an accredited school of public health, IUPUI is uniquely equipped to ensure a safe campus.
