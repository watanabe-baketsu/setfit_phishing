We'll meet you where you are.

With undergraduate and graduate campuses within 30 miles of every Pennsylvanian and globally online, Penn State's world-class education is available to meet students where they are.

Discover Penn State

Penn State is a top-ranked research university and Pennsylvania's sole land-grant institution, founded with a mission of high-quality teaching, expert research, and global service. Discover a community—more than 740,000 strong—driven to make a difference.

World-Class Education

With the resources of twenty-four campuses, plus an online World Campus, Penn State's educational model provides opportunities to build your future and your career.

Perspective: Leaders at Penn State

In a video series, “Perspective: Leaders at Penn State”, Kimberly A. Lawless, Dean of Penn State’s College of Education, reflects on her passion for remaking furniture while sharing her approach to creating classrooms where students can discover and embrace their unique talents.

The following video shows footage of Kimberly Lawless in a workshop, sanding and painting a wooden drawer with the words “Perspective: Leaders at Penn State” appearing on screen. The video then transitions to scenes of Kimberly talking to camera in a studio. Interspersed are photographs of Kimberly as a child, scenes of her refurbishing a wooden chest of drawers with a mirror, masked students outside on the grass and talking in classrooms, Kimberly talking at the front of a classroom. The video closes with Kimberly standing in a woodshop, surrounded by tools and a chest of drawers.
