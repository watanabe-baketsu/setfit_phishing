Our recent awards and prizes

The outstanding work of our journalists has been recognized with numerous awards. These include 56 Pulitzer Prizes, more than any other news organization in the categories for which we can compete.

2021 Pulitzer Prizes

In 2021, we won both the feature photography and breaking news photography awards.

2021 Emmy Awards

In 2021, we won the Engineering and Technology Award for our newsroom software, AP ENPS.

NPPA’s 2021 Best of Photojournalism contest

In 2021, AP photographers were honored with 19 National Press Photographers Association awards including best of show and first prize in the breaking news and feature photography categories.
