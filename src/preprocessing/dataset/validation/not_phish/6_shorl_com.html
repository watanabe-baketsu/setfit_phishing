To make a long story short: Shorl makes long URLs short.

Get right to it:


First time here?

  1. Enter a long URL in the form field above.
  2. Click the "Shorlify!" button, or press Enter on your keyboard.
  3. Voila! A shorl is created. Write down or memorize the password if you want access to the usage statistics for your shorl later on.

Got spam?

We hate spam. Shorls that are used in any form of spam will be disabled. Please report any misuse here or by email at abuse@shorl.com.
