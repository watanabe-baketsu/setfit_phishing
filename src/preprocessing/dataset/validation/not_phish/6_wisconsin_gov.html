Governor Tony Evers

As governor, Tony Evers will focus on ending the divisiveness that has overtaken the politics in the state. He will work with Republicans and Democrats to find solutions to the problems facing Wisconsin citizens. He believes that every single person has value, and if we are going to change the direction of our state, we must do it together.
