Welcome

Clark Moody Bitcoin is a growing collection of Bitcoin tools and information. The original version of this website goes back to 2011 with the launch of the first real-time market data site in the Bitcoin space. Back then, Mt. Gox was the only show in town, and Clark Moody Bitcoin brought interactive charts and a live order book to the crypto trader.

Now we're back and more excited than ever about the future of Bitcoin and the entire ecosystem.
